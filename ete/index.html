THE ÉTÉ HOUSE

Founded in 2016, by two dessert connoisseurs.

OUR PRODUCES

été Ice cream is one of the best products we produce that highlights ete’s commitment to simple artisan food products from our community of family farmers. Made with a high-fat base of cream and milk from our grass fed cows and simple ingredients this ice cream will delight all of your senses! As with all of our products we add no color to the ice cream. We stick with simple ingredients and are creating flavors that blend traditional old-fashion flavors with newest creative twists.

Highlights:

- Made with Organic milk from grass fed cows
- Non-GMO Project Verified
- Super Premium, high-fat ice cream
- No Carrageenan
- No artificial flavors or added colors
- We offer a blend of traditional and creative flavors
